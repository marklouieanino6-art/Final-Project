About the Province

The Gateway to Bicol's Hidden Gems

Camarines Norte, fondly called "CamNorte," is a first-class province in the Bicol Region of the Philippines. Known as the "Gold Province," it boasts pristine coastlines, rich mineral deposits, and a deeply rooted cultural heritage.

Location

Northernmost province of Bicol Region (Region V), bounded by Quezon Province to the north, Camarines Sur to the south, and the Pacific Ocean to the east.

Background & History

Established as an independent province in 1919, Daet is the provincial capital. The province gained prominence during the Spanish era for its gold mining industry along the Paracale coast.

Culture & Traditions

Rich Bicolano traditions, including the Kalilayan Festival, unique weaving crafts, and strong Catholic heritage are celebrated year-round.
